We will propose suitable personnel for various human resource needs of customers. We carefully follow up with companies and dispatched employees. Because we follow up carefully, the following problems that are common in foreign dispatch are unlikely to occur. Communication is the most common reason why dispatched staff quit soon. They get tired of their work because they do not improve. They are isolated because they cannot communicate well with employees and colleagues. They are dissatisfied with the workplace or have something to say but cannot say it. All of these are caused by a lack of communication. We carefully follow up with dispatched staff, such as individual counseling, and create an environment where dispatched staff can always approach their work with a smile. We also carefully follow up with companies, so if you have any requests or dissatisfaction with dispatched staff, such as language barriers, please feel free to tell our staff. We will promptly resolve language problems in each country and convey your company's requests to dispatched staff.
